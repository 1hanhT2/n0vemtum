It isn't just you. You have correctly identified a tonal clash.

The current copy leans heavily into **"Main Character Energy"** (RP/Roleplay) rather than **"Utility."** While the *Solo Leveling* inspiration is cool, using words like "Existence," "Transcend," and "Simulation" for a productivity app can feel heavy-handed, melodramatic, and yes—a bit pretentious.

Here is a breakdown of why it feels off and how to fix it while keeping the "System" identity.

### 1. The Problem: "Lore" vs. Clarity

In UI/UX, clarity is king. When a user reads "Optimize your existence," their brain has to pause and decode the metaphor. When they read "Track your habits," they know exactly what the app does.

Currently, the copy sounds like a sci-fi movie trailer.

* **"Transcend your limits"**: Vague. Does it mean I work harder? Run faster?
* **"Operatives"**: This implies the user is a spy or a soldier. Unless this is a game, it feels forced.
* **"Simulation"**: This implies real life isn't real. That’s a bit too philosophical for a Tuesday morning to-do list.

### 2. The Solution: "Lore-Lite"

You want the **aesthetic** of the System (cold, precise, analytical) without the **roleplay**.
Keep the *vocabulary* technical (Metrics, Data, Cycle, Sync), but drop the *drama* (Existence, Fate, Transcend).

### 3. Rewriting the Copy (Before vs. After)

**Headline**

* *Current:* "Optimize your existence within The System." (Too grand)
* *Better:* **"Build the System. Master your discipline."** (Strong, active, grounded)
* *Alternative:* **"Quantify your growth."** (Cold, analytical, fits the vibe)

**Sub-headline**

* *Current:* "Submit your daily metrics. Analyze your performance. Transcend your limits through our advanced gamification protocols."
* *Better:* **"Log daily metrics. Analyze performance data. Elevate your baseline through structured gamification."**
* *Why:* "Elevate your baseline" is technical. "Transcend limits" is mystical.



**Call to Action (Buttons)**

* *Current:* "Begin Initialization" / "Observe Simulation"
* *Better:* **"Initialize System"** / **"View Demo"**
* *Why:* "Initialize" is great tech jargon. "Simulation" is confusing—are we watching a video or testing the app?



**Social Proof**

* *Current:* "Utilized by operatives at..."
* *Better:* **"Deployed by high-performers at..."** or **"Trusted by builders at..."**
* *Why:* "Operatives" is the biggest offender here. "High-performers" fits the *Solo Leveling* ethos (getting stronger) without pretending to be a hunter.



### Summary

To fix the "pretentious" feel, simply **lower the stakes**. You aren't saving the world or rewriting reality; you are optimizing a workflow.
